About Us

Happy Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing education, healthcare, and empowerment to underprivileged children and families in India. Our mission is to transform lives and create a brighter future for those in need.

Our Services

Free Education for Underprivileged Children

Our organization provides free education to children who lack access to quality schooling. We believe that education is the key to a brighter future for all.

Elderly Care and Support

We care for and support the elderly in our community, ensuring they live with dignity and receive the healthcare they deserve.

Empowering Women through Employment

We empower women by providing employment opportunities, skills training, and support to become self-reliant and independent.
